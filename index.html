USER GUIDE

To access control panel; tap or hover toward bottom of player screen.

To play and pause video; click anywhere on the upper part of the player screen, or you can simply use the play button.

To mute and unmute video; click on the speaker button, or click anywhere on the volume bar to unmute.

To adjust video volume; click anywhere on the volume bar.

To seek or skip to anywhere in the video; simply click anywhere on the progress bar.

To go in and out of fullscreen mode; double click anywhere on the upper part of the player screen, or you can simply use the fullscreen button.

To adjust video speed; click the Playback speed button and select your desired speed rate from the options.
